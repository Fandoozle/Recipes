Chicken Soup

Ingredients:

- 1 onion, diced
- 2 celery stalks, sliced
- 1 cup of carrot shreds
- 2 pounds of chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 3 chicken bouillon cubes
- 12 cups of water or chicken stock
- 1 teaspoon of salt
- 1/4 teaspoon of pepper
- 2 cups of egg noodles
- 2 cans (14.75 ounces each) of creamed corn
- 1/4 cup of butter

Directions:

1. In a large pot, combine the onion, celery, carrot, chicken, bouillon cubes, water or stock, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat, then lower the heat and simmer, uncovered, for 30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are soft.
2. Stir in the egg noodles, creamed corn, and butter. Cook, uncovered, for another 10 minutes, or until the noodles are tender, stirring occasionally.
