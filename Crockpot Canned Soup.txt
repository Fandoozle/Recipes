Crockpot Canned Soup

Ingredients:

- 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
- 1 can (6 ounces) of tomato paste
- 1 can of carrots, sliced and drained
- 1 can (15 ounces) of green beans, drained
- 1 can (15 ounces) of peas, drained
- 1 can (15 ounces) of corn, drained
- 1 can (15 ounces) of potatoes, diced and drained
- 2 cartons (32 ounces each) of garden vegetable broth
- 1 teaspoon of dried basil
- 1 teaspoon of dried onion flakes
- 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon of salt
- 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
- 2 tablespoons of Italian seasoning

Directions:

1. In a large crockpot, combine the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, carrots, green beans, peas, corn, potatoes, broth, basil, onion, garlic, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Stir well to mix everything together.
2. Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours, or on high for 3 to 4 hours, until the vegetables are tender.
3. Enjoy your crockpot canned soup!
