Lentil Soup

Ingredients:

- 1 cup of dried lentils, rinsed and drained
- 1 cup of celery, diced
- 1 cup of carrots, diced
- 1 cup of onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, peeled and minced
- 1/2 teaspoon of dried basil
- 1/4 teaspoon of dried thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
- 1 bay leaf
- 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
- 4 cups of chicken broth
- 1 cup of water
- 1/2 cup of tomato sauce or 1 tomato, chopped
- 1 turnip, peeled and diced

Directions:

1. In a large pot, combine the lentils, celery, carrots, onion, garlic, basil, thyme, oregano, bay leaf, pepper, broth, water, and tomato sauce or tomato. Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat, then lower the heat and simmer, covered, for 45 minutes, or until the lentils are soft and tender.
2. Add the turnip and continue to simmer, uncovered, for another 15 minutes, or until the turnip is cooked through.
