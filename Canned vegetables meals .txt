Easy Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients:

    1 can of already drained mixed vegetables
    Crushed garlic
    A stick of melted butter
    3 cups of white/brown rice

Instructions:

    In a saucepan saute in the vegetables, garlic and melted butter.
    Once the vegetables are cooked, add in three cups of cooked rice, saute with the vegetables.
    For taste, add in seasonings, salt, pepper and chilli flakes.
    Plate the fried rice and enjoy with any protein dish.

Garlic Butter Mixed Vegetables

Ingredients:

    A can of mixed vegetables
    Half a cup vegetable broth
    A tbsp butter
    A tsp of garlic (minced)
    Black pepper
    2 tbsp of Parmesan cheese (grated)
    1 tbsp fresh parsley (chopped) for garnish

Instructions:

    Mix in the vegetables and broth together in a medium saucepan over medium heat and let it cook for 5 to 10 minutes.
    Drain off the broth and then add in the butter, garlic, pepper and parmesan cheese. And mix until well blended.
    To finish, sprinkle each serving with freshly chopped parsley for added color.

Canned Corn and Black Bean Salad

Ingredients:

- 1 can (15 ounces) of corn, drained
- 1 can (15 ounces) of black beans, rinsed and drained
- 1 red onion, chopped
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 1 avocado, peeled and chopped
- 1/4 cup of fresh or dried cilantro, chopped
- 1 lime, juiced
- Salt, pepper, and cumin, to taste

Directions:

1. In a large bowl, toss together the corn, black beans, onion, bell pepper, avocado, cilantro, and lime juice.
2. Season with salt, pepper, and cumin, according to your preference.
3. Refrigerate the salad for an hour to let the flavors meld.
4. Serve cold or at room temperature, as a side dish or a main course.

Canned Tomato and Basil Soup

Ingredients:

- 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
- 1 can (6 ounces) of tomato paste
- 1 can (32 ounces) of chicken or vegetable broth
- 1/4 cup of fresh or dried basil
- Salt, pepper, and garlic powder, to taste

Directions:

1. In a blender or food processor, puree the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, broth, and basil until smooth and well combined.
2. Transfer the soup to a large pot and bring it to a boil over high heat.
3. Reduce the heat and simmer the soup for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder, according to your taste.
5. Serve hot, with crusty bread or grilled cheese sandwiches.

Canned Green Bean Casserole

Ingredients:

- 1 can (10.5 ounces) of cream of mushroom soup
- 1 can (10.5 ounces) of milk
- 1 teaspoon of soy sauce
- A dash of black pepper
- 1 can (14.5 ounces) of green beans, drained
- 1 can (6 ounces) of fried onions, divided

Directions:

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and spray a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the soup, milk, soy sauce, and pepper until smooth.
3. In a large bowl, toss together the green beans and half of the fried onions.
4. Spread the green bean mixture evenly over the prepared baking dish.
5. Pour the soup mixture over the green beans and spread it gently to cover them.
6. Bake the casserole for 25 minutes, or until bubbly.
7. Sprinkle the remaining fried onions over the top and bake for another 5 minutes, or until golden.
